## Synopsis
It’s a physics-based game where you have to fit and stack all your beach gear like chairs, coolers, and towels within the shadow of a parasol while following different objectives.

 Made for the Comfy Jam.

## How to play
- Click on the van to drop items

- Drag and drop the items that match with the instructions and take them to the shade!

- Verify your answer
